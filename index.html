ABOUT ME.

I'm a busy Generation Xer who has excelled in a career outside of tech, supported a family, raised a child, travelled the world, served my country, interacted with people from many walks of life, and now manages my own business. On any particular day, I have a long to do list.

I have an outsider's healthy skepticism of gimicky tech which detracts from the user's productivity, and is seemingly created in a vacuum with no input from the potential user. Tech should adapt to us and our needs, not the other way around. It should always improve a process, not create additional tasks. I allow these principles to guide me as I tackle each new computing challenge.
